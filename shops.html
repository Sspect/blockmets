🛒 Shop Building Guidelines

Hey everyone! 🌈 If you want to build a shop, here’s what you need to know:

  1. 🏝️ Shop Island
    Your shop should be built on the Shop Island to keep things organized and easy to find.
    Travel there by typing: /warp shops

    You’re free to build a shop even if you're unsure how active you'll be.
    If you stop playing, it's okay to leave it unstocked or pass it on to someone else.
  2. 🗺️ Viewing Chunks
    To see chunk borders, press the following keys at the same time:
    - Windows: F3 + G
    - Mac: FN + F3 + G
    Don’t hesitate to ask for help if you’re unsure!
  3. 🏠 Claim Your Space
    You can claim as many chunks as you need for your shop.
    Just make sure your chunks are at least one chunk away from any other shop.
    If you want to be closer, ask the nearest shop owner for permission.
  4. 🚗 Roads and Spacing
    The chunks between shops will be used as roads.
    Please keep your shop close to others (while still respecting the one-chunk gap).
    This helps create a nice and navigable area for everyone.
  5. 📚 Let People Know
    It’s a good idea to place a lectern with a book and quill in or near your shop.
    You can use it to share:
    • Who built the shop
    • What it sells
    • How often you plan to restock
